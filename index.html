ABOUT




Magical Moments Catering & Event Planning is a full service catering and event planning company with first class experience. We are currently serving Eastern North Carolina and Southeastern Virgina. We offer personalized service which includes consultations to initiate a relationship with each customer. Our job is to listen to your needs and wants in return we will work closely with you to bring your vision to life!


Magical Moments is here to assist you with all of your event planning needs, from beginning to end! Our team is knowledgeable, creative and confident in our ability to make your event memorable. When you choose Magical Moments for your next event, you can rest assured that the event will be stress-free, exceptional and delicately executed! We offer full service for the following events:


  • WEDDINGS
  • ANNIVERSARIES
  • CORPORATE EVENTS
  • HOLIDAY CELEBRATIONS
  • SCHOOL FUNCTIONS(including proms & spring dances)
  • GRADUATIONS
  • FAMILY REUNIONS
  • CHURCH CELEBRATIONS
  • BABY SHOWERS
  • OFFICE MEETINGS
  • BRIDAL SHOWERS
  • ENGAGEMENT PARTIES
  • COCKTAIL & DINNER PARTIES
